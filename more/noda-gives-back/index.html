Community Is Everything

Greater New Orleans is an incredible area that has given all of us so much joy and fun. From the music to the festivals. The swamps to the crawfish. Carnival and beyond. We have been fortunate to be part of one of the most unique historical and cultural regions in the world. It is imperative that we do our part in maintaining the wonders of our home, and to help those less fortunate in being able to do so as well. NODA is committed to doing its part in those efforts, and we can only dream of being able to do a fraction of the good deeds that so many others have done for our community.

37 FOR 37 DODGEBALL TOURNAMENT

Our dear friend, Molly, came up with the idea for this event, to raise some money for Team Gleason.

Since NODA first took to the courts back in 2014, it has helped with numerous service projects, such as: Second Harvest Food Drive Open Gym, 37 For 37 Dodgeball Tournament to benefit Team Gleason, multiple trips to the New Orleans Mission, Boo At the Zoo, NORDC Youth Fit Fest, a charity dodgeball tournament to benefit a Metairie native who was injured in the Orlando Nightclub shooting, several Son of a Saint events, and more.
